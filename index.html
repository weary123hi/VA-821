WHO WE ARE

Established in 1982, we were one of the first AFJROTC units in the state. Cadets of VA-821 come from three Arlington High Schools, Arlington alternative secondary programs, and George Mason high school in Falls Church. We also take pride in our diversity here as cadets are diverse in both culture and ethnicity. Welcome to VA-821!

MERCH STORE

Here you'll find clothes both in style and in regulations! Cadet's can choose to buy optional gear that the logistics team doesn't provide. Friends and family are also welcome to purchase gear in representation of their fellow cadet!

ACTIVITIES

Our cadets abide by the Cadet Creed, part of the cadet creed reads "I earn respect when I uphold the core values of integrity first, service before self, and excellence in all we do." Our cadets embody this creed through many ways, but the most prominent of which would be community service. Our cadets are constantly serving the citizens of Arlington whether it be by way of performing the colors, lending a hand, or hosting and serving an event. We make it our mission to meet a minimum of 1500 community service hours each year, and we always find ourselves demolishing that very goal.

CONTACT US

As mentioned prior, we abide by the Cadet Creed and AFJROTC/VA-821 mission, so our cadets are constantly on the look out for opportunities.
